Welcome to the unofficial website for A$AP Rocky. Rakim Athelaston Mayers, known professionally as ASAP Rocky, is an American rapper, singer, and record producer. Stay up to date with all new music drops, tour and event information, and new merch drops! A$AP Rocky is a record-winning rapper artist and producer hailing from Harlem New York. He is a part of the A$AP Mob collective. A$AP, which stands for "Always Strive and Prosper," is the prefix of all the members' stage names. In addition to his music, Rocky is a fashion icon donning collaborations with multiple high-end fashion brands and streetwear.
